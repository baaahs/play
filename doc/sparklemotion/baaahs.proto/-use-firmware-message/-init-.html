UseFirmwareMessage(url: String)

The message that Pinky will send to a brain when Pinky has decided that the Brain should use a particular firmware. The url can point anywhere, either self hosted by Pinky or out into the nether reaches of the Interwebs. What could possibly go wrong? Pinky would never tell a brain to go download a wikipedia article and use that as a firmware. It just won't be nice.
